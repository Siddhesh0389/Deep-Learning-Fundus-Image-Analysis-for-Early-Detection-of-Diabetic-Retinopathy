Diabetic Retinopathy

Diabetic retinopathy is a serious eye complication caused by diabetes. It damages the blood vessels of the retina and may lead to vision loss if not detected early. Proper monitoring and early diagnosis are essential to prevent blindness.

ABOUT PROJECT

Problem

Diabetic Retinopathy (DR) is a common complication of diabetes that causes lesions on the retina and affects vision. If not detected early, it can lead to permanent blindness. Manual diagnosis is time-consuming and expensive.

Solution

This project uses Transfer Learning with Deep Learning to automatically detect and classify stages of Diabetic Retinopathy. Users can upload retinal images and instantly receive predictions through a web interface.
